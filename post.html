Lorem Picsum

Lorem Picsum is similar to lorem ipsum, but instead of filler text, it provides filler photos. You can click on the images to visit their site. They offer many options, including random, and grayscale images. The cool thing is, you can determine the height and width of the images provided. Check out Picsum for yourself and explore some neat photos!
